Connect Galileo to Wifi

This will let Galileo connect to your home wifi network.

Windows users will need to make sure they have Bonjour before proceeding.

*nix users will need to make sure they have mDNS before proceeding. Essentially they must be able to ping <hostname>.local addresses. See: wikipedia introduction , Avahi , Zeroconf .

Check to make sure your OS has mDNS before proceeding.
